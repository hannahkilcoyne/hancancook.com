**  
Roasted Broccoli**  
  
2 broccoli crowns, washed, dried, and cut into thin, even pieces  
Juice of 1/2 lemon  
3 tbsp olive oil  
Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste  
  
Preheat the oven to 450Â° and place two large baking sheets inside. Add the broccoli to a mixing bowl and coat with lemon juice, olive oil, salt and pepper. Pour the broccoli out evenly onto the preheated baking sheets. Roast for 25-30 minutes, until broccoli begins to brown.
