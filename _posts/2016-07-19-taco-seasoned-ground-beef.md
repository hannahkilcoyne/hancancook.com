## Ingredients

- 1 lb lean ground beef
- 1/2 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tbsp smoked paprika
- Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Preheat a skillet on medium heat. Add the ground beef, onion powder, garlic powder, cumin and paprika. Salt and pepper generously. Saut√© the beef until it is browned.
