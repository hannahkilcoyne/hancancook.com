## Ingredients

- 1 bottle of red wine (I used a red blend)
- 4 cups apple cider
- 2 cinnamon sticks
- 4 whole cloves
- Zest of one lime
- Juice of one lime
- 1/4 cup sugar (optional)

## Instructions

1. Mix all ingredients together in a large saucepan. Bring to a boil and them simmer on medium for 10 minutes before serving.
